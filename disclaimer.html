Important information regarding our digital heritage platform.

Educational Purposes

This website is a digital heritage project created for educational and cultural preservation purposes. It is not the official government portal for the Rizal Shrine Dapitan City, but a dedicated platform to enhance public awareness and historical appreciation.

Accuracy of Information

While we strive for historical accuracy, we recommend consulting official National Historical Commission of the Philippines (NHCP) documents for academic or legal research.

Intellectual Property

All heritage assets, photographs, and historical data remain the property of their respective owners and the Philippine government. This platform aims to showcase these treasures in a modern, accessible format.
