About the Shrine

A National Historical Landmark of the Philippines

A Living Testament

The Legend of Talisay

From 1892 to 1896, Dr. Jose Rizal spent his most productive years in this 16-hectare sanctuary.

The shrine is more than a garden; it is a reconstruction of the original estate purchased by Rizal with his lottery winnings. Each structure tells a story of his daily life, his commitment to the community, and his unwavering love for his country.

Science & Nature

Rizal discovered new species of beetles, frogs, and lizards during his time in Talisay, leaving a massive mark on Filipino biology.

Impact

Education

Establishing his own school, he taught local boys not only academics but also martial arts, swimming, and agriculture.

Service

Medicine

As an ophthalmic surgeon, he treated thousands of patients, often for free, including those traveling across the ocean to see him.
